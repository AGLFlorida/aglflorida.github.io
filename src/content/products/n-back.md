Dual N-Back is a cognitive training game based on the classic N-Back task—a research-backed method for improving working memory and fluid intelligence. The game challenges you to track a sequence of spoken letters and grid positions, identifying when each matches what appeared N steps earlier.

Built for people who want a clean, distraction-free training tool—especially useful for those looking to sharpen short-term memory and focus. Multiple game modes let you adjust intensity: go full Dual N-Back for maximum challenge, or isolate auditory or visual stimuli to target specific skills.

Difficulty adapts as you improve, keeping sessions challenging without being discouraging.
